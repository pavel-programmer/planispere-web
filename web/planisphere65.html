How it works:

On the edges of the map the months and dates are marked. On the edges of the circle hours and minutes are marked. To set the planisphere for a specific date and time, you need to match the date on the map with the time on the circle, and also make an adjustment for local time.

First, set the observation date and time to within 10 minutes, then click the button.

If your coordinates and time zone are entered correctly, congratulations — the planisphere is ready and shows the night sky.

For more convenient observation of the stars, use the button to adjust the map according to the cardinal directions. Just stand facing the wished direction and tap the corresponding button.

If you want to slightly adjust the result shown by the map, use the button. This is especially useful if you have not entered your coordinates and time zone or have done it incorrectly.
